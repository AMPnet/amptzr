How to distribute dividends or make airdrops?
Airdropping tokens or distributing dividends to a certain group of people by using Dev3 is a very straightforward process.
1. Create a snapshot of token holders (e.g. snapshot of all people who held $MKR token on July 1st, 2022)
2. Decide who to exclude from the snapshot (e.g. exclude the address of founders or big investors, if rewarding smaller holders)
3. Go to 'Token Snapshots' tab and select 'Airdrop Tokens'
