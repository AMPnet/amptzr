Airdropping tokens & distributing dividends
Aidropper -> This module enables you to create gas-efficient airdrops of any ERC-20 token to holders of any other ERC-20 token.

The airdrops work by creating snapshots, which are lists of holders of some ERC-20 token at a specific moment in time. Once you have a snapshot, you can use it as many times as you want to airdrop tokens to wallets contained in the snapshot.
